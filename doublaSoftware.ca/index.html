Who am I? My name is Alec Arakilyan. I am a Software Developer born in Montréal, Québec. My favorite way to express art is through video games because they incorporate art forms such as visual, storytelling, music, etc. I graduated from Concordia University with a bachelor's in Computer Science. I have successfully contributed to 2 mobile games ( Fun with Prepositions and Oblivion). Music is a large part of my life as well. I saw many metal bands play live before the pandemic, and I hope to see more soon. My favorite instruments to play are guitar and keyboard. In recent years, Cryptocurrencies have risen to the public eye. I used to mine Ethereum until the hashrate increased to an amount where I was no longer making a profit. Now, I mine Ravencoin with my gaming PC when I'm not using it.
